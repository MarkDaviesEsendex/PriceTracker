Product Description


Large laundry loads are no problem for the Indesit IDV75 Tumble Dryer as it has an impressive 7 kilogram of capacity. With many useful features like timed drying, refresh cycle and reverse action drum, the Indesit IDV75 Tumble Dryer also includes different programmes. With B energy rating, a quiet 69dB noise level and the convenience of easy to use knob control, the Indesit IDV75 Tumble Dryer can be trusted to deliver on time, every time. The White coloured Indesit IDV75 Tumble Dryer has it all.
