Product Description

The HUAWEI MateBook X Pro (MateBook X Pro for short) is a 13.9-inch ultra-slim laptop that incorporates a 8th Generation Intel Core i5 8250U processor and supports high-speed Wi-Fi Internet.

The MateBook X Pro has a slim metallic body that is 14.6 mm thick, making it both stylish and easy to carry. The MateBook X Pro has a 3K display with a 3000 x 2000 pixel LTPS touchscreen, 100 percent sRGB colour gamut, and maximum brightness of 450 nits.

The screen-to-body ratio is 91 percent, and the viewing angle can reach an impressive 178 degrees. It is also equipped with a 1 MP front recessed camera.

The MateBook X Pro is a smart work assistant, supporting Office 365 Personal (30 days trial) and 4K video playback.

The MateBook X Pro adopts a USB-C charging port that enables the cable to be inserted with either side facing up. You can charge your MateBook X Pro, transfer data, and use DisplayPort with the two USB-C ports on the left side. The Thunderbolt 3 port (compatible with USB-C) provides a faster transfer speed, and your Thunderbolt USB device is only compatible with the Thunderbolt 3 port.
